Rusted Vengence OST This was a large scale game that I worked on with the Illinois State University Game Development Club, where I worked with the music team, lead by Tommy Harris. The game is a mixture of an RPG and a fighting game, with an overworld and a seperate battle arena. This was my first time working in a much larger crew on a full scale game, and it was a great experience collaborating with the people in this project to make the music for this game. Be on the lookout in the future for a new game from the Game Dev Club in the coming months, as I returned again to make music for the upcoming spring 2022 game.
